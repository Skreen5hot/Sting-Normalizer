Wikidata Suggestions

The library never calls Wikidata. When this toggle is on, the harness detects acronym-shaped tokens in your schema keys, queries query.wikidata.org for matching P1813 short-name entries, and lets you pick which expansion (if any) to feed back into the abbreviation dictionary.
